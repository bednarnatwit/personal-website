Research (ongoing)

Quantum Cosmology Finite Velocity Diffusion

Research Overview

Implement a specific factor ordering of the Wheeler-DeWitt equation to conduct numerical simulations, enabling extrapolation of data on the universe's state near the singularity.

Real World Significance

By investigating the Wheeler-DeWitt equation near the singularity, we may be able to provide insights into the universe's origins, quantum gravity, and the nature of the Big Bang, advancing our understanding of fundamental physics as a whole.

Personal Contributions (thus far)

Analyze relationships between key variables in the Wheeler-DeWitt equation, apply second-order partial derivative approximations to the chosen factor ordering, and run baseline numerical simulations in MATLAB using early data.
